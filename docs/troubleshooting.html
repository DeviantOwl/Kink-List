I want to restart!

Method 1: Clearing your history. Sometimes local storage is denoted under the "Advance Clean" and is not cleaned by default and must be ticked by the user in the process.

Method 2: You can use your browser's Dev console to clear the local storage. Important: You must be on the page deviantbound.com AND not on selection screen as it can cause issues. Instead do this on result page to prevent issues. For Chrome: press F12 (typically) and go to Application > Local Storage (Then Right Click Clear). Firefox is similar but calls the tab Storage instead of application.

Method 2: Use Incognito on your browser.

Why is one kink always selected?

Due to how the site works it defaults to needing something to be selected, therfore all users must choose their opinion of being dominant. If you really wish not to select this you can set the local storage entry called r_rating_dominant_XXXXXX all to false (Like using method 2 above). I suggest doing this on the results page (rather than selection page), then refresh the results page.
